Hired items must be paid for when booking.
 
 The hire period includes the three days the Hirer receives the goods prior to their trip 
 and the three days after the trip, before the goods are returned to Ski-Stuff (in all 13 days).
 
 Goods will arrive at the address provided by the Hirer three days prior to the date given by the Hirer for the start of their trip.
 
 Goods must be returned within 3 days of the date given for the end of the Hirers trip 
 and at the cost of the Hirer. Royal Mail will provide a proof of postage label which 
 should be kept safe until you have received confirmation that we have received and checked the items and the deposit is returned to you. 
 
 Please do not wash any of the hired items. All clothes which are hired are laundered on return to Ski-Stuff.
 
 The Hirer accepts full personal liability for any loss or damage to goods during the hire period. Insurance is up to the Hirer.
 The Hirer accepts full responsibility for any goods hired even if they are used by other members of the party.
 
 Ski-Stuff does not take any reponsibility for items which may not fit. The sizing chart 
 and sizing advice we provide should be used when hiring goods.
  
Vallåssen:

Vilkår for skiudlejning
  For at kunne leje udstyr på Vallåsen, skal du afgive personlige oplysninger. Vallåsen skal vide, hvem lejeaftalen indgås med, og hvor man kan finde kunden, hvis for eksempel udstyret ikke tilbageleveres. Al håndtering af personlige oplysninger foregår i henhold til den svenske persondatalov, Personuppgiftslagen (PuL).
Når du lejer dit udstyr, skal du kunne legitimere dig med gyldig fotolegitimation. En person i et selskab kan tage ansvaret for flere sæt udstyr. Du har ansvaret for skader på udstyr, som er opstået på grund af uforsigtighed. Kan du ikke levere dit udstyr tilbage, er du erstatningspligtig over for Vallåsen. Den, som har ansvaret for udstyr, skal være mindst 15 år.
